# June 2021 ISC Forensic Contest (AgentTesla + Hancitor/Cobalt Strike/Ficker + Qakbot)
# Multi-host infection: 3 infected machines, 5 malware families
# One question per line

What are the IP addresses of the potentially infected internal hosts in the LAN?
What are the hostnames of the potentially infected machines in the LAN?
What are the Windows human user account names (not machine accounts ending in $) of the potentially infected machines in the LAN?
What external domains or IP addresses are associated with the initial infection and malware delivery?
What are the suspicious external IP addresses contacted, which might be involved in command-and-control (C2) communication?
During what hour did the first signs of infection activity appear? Provide the earliest date and time in YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC format.
Were any suspicious certificates observed in the encrypted network traffic? If so, identify the domain or IP in the certificate.
According to the connection volume statistics, what single external IP address received the most connections from the infected hosts? Is it a C2 server or a legitimate service?
